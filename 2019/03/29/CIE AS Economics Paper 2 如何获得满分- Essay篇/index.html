CIE AS Economics Paper 2 如何获得满分- Essay篇

发表于 2019-03-29 | 更新于 2019-04-06 | 分类于 考试攻略

大多数同学在学习A Level经济的过程中难免会感到Paper 2的essay部分难度较大，即便参照评分标准也很难写出各方面都符合要求的文章。但是最难的写作部分也是有一定的规律和套路的。废话不多说，我们直接上干货。

经过细致的统计，我发现历年出题频率最高的考点就是elasticity(弹性)，几乎每套试卷都出现过，题型也相对固定，part a考的基本是弹性的概念，part b是弹性的运用(包括enterprise和government)。所以在具体回答part a的问题之前，我们需要写出该种弹性的定义和计算公式，如果题干中只出现了一种弹性，如PED，我们除了写定义和公式以外，还需要解释elastic和inelsatic。Part b有可能出现的问题我做了如下的总结和归类：

1. PED(Price Elasticity of Demand)

  1. The usefulness of PED for entrepreneurs to increase revenue
  2. Why entrepreneurs may want to CHANGE the price elasticity of demand for their products
  3. The usefulness of PED for the government

2. YED(Income Elasticity of Demand)

  1. The usefulness of YED in predicting the change in a firm’s revenue
  2. The usefulness of YED for a business to increase its sales revenue
  3. The usefulness of YED for the government

3. XED(Cross Elasticity of Demand)

  1. The usefulness of XED in predicting the change in a firm’s revenue
  2. The usefulness of XED for a business to 3. increase its sales revenue
  3. The usefulness of XED for the government

4. PES(Price Elasticity of Supply)

  1. How governments might CHANGE the PES of a good

下面，我们来看一道历年真题：
9708-s16-22-Q3

我们在回答part a之前应该先写出PED的定义和公式(参考课本)，分值分别为1分，总共2分；由于这道题问的是哪些因素会导致对某种商品的需求更加富有弹性(relatively price-elastic)，所以我们只需要解释elastic: a change in the price of the good results in a greater percentage change in quantity demanded。这部分分值为2分。定义的部分总共为4分，可以写在一个段落当中。

接着我们要回答哪些因素导致PED更加elastic，这部分为4分。一个因素1分，对应的解释为1分，所以至少要写两个原因：

The number of substitutes a product has will affect its price elasticity of demand. The higher the number of substitutes, the more we would expect consumers to switch away from a particular product when its price goes up, therefore, the demand for the product tends to be more elastic.

The proportion of income spent on a product will (also) affect its price elasticity of demand. The higher the relative proportion of income accounted for by the product, the higher the impact will be on the consumer’s income level of a change in the product’s price, therefore, the demand for the product tends to be more elastic.

这样，一个满分的part a就完成了。

接着，我们来分析part b。Entrepreneur改变弹性的原因无外乎是为了获得更多的收入(revenue)，他即可能想办法提高弹性，这样就很容易通过适当降价来增加收入，也有可能降低弹性，通过提升价格来增加收入。前面的8分需要描述的是enterprise具体用什么办法来增加/降低弹性，目的和结果分别是怎样的：

Entrepreneurs tend to make the demand for the products more inelastic as it would be easier to increase revenue by raising their prices (an increase in price will result in a smaller percentage reduction in quantity demanded). This can be achieved by innovation, which will make their products more unique and different from those produced by their competitors, therefore, the number of substitutes becomes smaller. Entrepreneurs can also enhance the brand loyalty through advertising, therefore consumers will be more reluctant to reduce their consumption in case of a rise in price.

They might also want to make the demand for the products more elastic, as a small cut in price will result in an increase in revenue (a decrease in price will result in a greater percentage increase in quantity demanded). This can be achieved by producing more similar products, which will increase the number of substitutes.

接着就到了part b最难的部分 - Evaluation。这里我们要分析政府的操作什么时候有效，什么时候效果不好。这里没有固定的答案，主要从见效的时间和操作的难易程度和可能产生的成本来分别分析两种方法各自的优缺点。

最后，我对几种弹性的历年出题规律和对应的答题技巧做了一个总结，但这里只提供PED的部分，其他的部分只提供纸质版，望谅解。写作部分的问题欢迎在后台探讨。祝大家考试顺利！
